Puppy Love

Valerie Frederick

For me finding that perfect puppy should have been easy. I've never met a dog I couldn't love, so what could be so hard about finding that perfect soul mate of dogs? For me, that search for the perfect puppy was marred down in a long list of wants and don't wants. I wanted a Jack Russell Terrier, smooth coat, white with a little red color, small perfectly folded over triangle ears, with a cuddly personality.

Joy

After searching far and wide, I ended up finding a not so perfect Jack Russell mix. 4 months old, no social skills, mouthy, rough coat, more red than white fur, ears that stood up and were 5 times too big for her head and nobody wanted her. Sometimes the universe gives you what you need, not what you want, and at that time, I was mourning the loss of my other Jack Russell, Dreamer, and I needed this crazy, silly not so perfect puppy and she needed me! 10 years later, I couldn't be happier with my crazy, bossy, little girl named Joy.
